# Hospital-Management
HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is for computerizing the working in a hospital. It is capable to provide easy and effective storage of information related to hospital ie patient’s data, doctor’s information, billing information, medicines prescribed to patient etc .It is designed to improve the quality and management of hospital . Hospital Information Systems provide a common source of information about a patient’s health history. The system have to keep data in secure place and controls who can reach the data in certain circumstances. These systems enhance the ability of health care professionals to coordinate care by providing a patient’s health information and visit history at the place and time that it is needed. It improves the effectiveness and quality of work. It enhances the patient care and increases the profitability of the organization. The interface is very user-friendly.  It helps to view a broad picture of hospital growth Improved monitoring of drug usage, and study of effectiveness. It Enhances information integrity, reduces transcription errors, and reduces duplication of information entries The data are well protected for personal use and makes the data processing very fast.
